Internship

Looking for critical thinkers!

We are looking to mentor critical thinkers in solving real-life problems with complex solutions, across all domains of knowledge, in a professional research environment.

Abstract

  • Rs. 5000 monthly stipend
  • Solutions are directly applied across all levels of the company
  • Precursor to a possible long-term employment
  • Performance bonus

Duration

3 months, extendable up to 6.

Mode

We operate out of TIFR-Hyderabad, but we welcome work-from-home applicants provided they have a reliable internet connection.

Instructions for Essay Questions

  1. Write in depth and/or breadth.
  2. Create a logical narrative with your writing.
  3. Write each sentence with intention. Know why you’re writing what you’re writing.
  4. No word limit.

Application Strategy

Copy-paste the questions to a word document; work on it for a week or two. Paste your response back into this form to submit your response.

Roles and Responsibilities

You would be

  • required to think critically, clearly and deeply.
  • responsible for finding solutions to an independent problem.

Conclusion

The goal of our company is to

  • create a work culture of debate, discussion, empathy and kindness
  • free up labor in the lab
  • increase researcher productivity
  • create an industry of lab automation

We aim to achieve our goal by

  • fundamentally re-examining the philosophy of {biology, experimentation, …}
  • building the next generation of experimental tools
  • capturing and transforming human intelligence into machine intelligence

We believe that achieving this goal is important for society’s progress.
